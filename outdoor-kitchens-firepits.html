Outdoor Kitchens Designed for Real Backyard Cooking

A well-designed outdoor kitchen makes cooking outside effortless. Instead of a single grill sitting on the patio, a built-in kitchen creates a complete cooking space with preparation areas, storage, and room for guests to gather while food is being prepared.

Many Denver homeowners are choosing outdoor kitchens because they allow them to enjoy Colorado’s mild summer evenings without being stuck indoors preparing meals.

Built-In Grills

Professional-grade grills built directly into stone or masonry structures create a permanent cooking station that becomes the centerpiece of the backyard.

Prep & Serving Space

Countertops and prep areas make it easy to prepare food, serve guests, and keep everything organized during gatherings.

Outdoor Appliances

Refrigerators, sinks, and storage cabinets allow your outdoor kitchen to function just like an indoor kitchen.

Backyard Firepits That Bring People Together

Firepits are one of the most popular outdoor features for Denver backyards. They provide warmth during cool evenings and create a natural place for friends and family to gather.

Whether built into a patio or installed as a focal point in the yard, a firepit adds both atmosphere and function to your outdoor living space.

  • Built-in stone firepits
  • Gas firepit installation with clean-burning flames
  • Wood-burning firepits for traditional fires
  • Seating walls surrounding the fire area
  • Fire features integrated into patios or outdoor kitchens

Materials Built for Colorado Weather

Outdoor kitchens and fire features must handle intense sun, freezing temperatures, snow, and seasonal weather changes. We use durable materials designed to perform well in Denver’s high-altitude climate.

  • Natural stone and masonry for long-lasting fire features
  • Granite and stone countertops that resist outdoor conditions
  • Stainless steel appliances designed specifically for outdoor kitchens
  • Gas line fire systems for reliable ignition and consistent flames
  • Weather-resistant construction built for Colorado’s temperature swings

Free Quote

If so, we schedule a free on-site visit where we walk your yard together — assessing grade, sun exposure, views, snow accumulation zones, and potential features. 3D Design & Quote
