How it works

From First Call to First Barbecue

Every home is a canvas. Here's our proven four-step process — established over 15 years and 500+ Colorado builds.

1

Free Consultation

We start with a quick phone call to discuss your project and see if we're a good fit. If so, we schedule a free on-site visit where we walk your yard together — assessing grade, sun exposure, views, snow accumulation zones, and potential features. This is where design ideas and budgets are carefully considered. No pressure, no commitment required.

2

3D Design & Quote

Your designer returns with a full photorealistic 3D rendering of your deck from every angle, plus physical material samples and a complete itemized fixed-price proposal. Our technology removes all visualization barriers. You'll see exactly what your outdoor space will look like before a single board is cut.

3

Permits & Schedule

We're completely turnkey. Our office handles the framing plans, structural engineering, permit applications, HOA submissions, and material take-off. You get a firm written start date and completion window before we touch your yard. A dedicated project manager keeps you informed throughout — no wondering what's happening.

4

Build & Walk-Through

Our team builds with minimal disruption to your daily life. The same crew you met early in the process follows through to completion. When we're done, we do a full walk-through together — nothing is signed off until you've reviewed every detail and you're satisfied. Then it's time to host.
