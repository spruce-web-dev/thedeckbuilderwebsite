Composite Deck Materials: Beauty + Low Maintenance

The material you choose defines your deck’s longevity, upkeep, and visual appeal. We guide you through each premium composite tier, balancing aesthetics with Colorado’s high-altitude demands to deliver a deck that stays stunning year after year.

Premium Capped Composite

High-performance composite core wrapped in a durable polymer cap layer. Resists fading, staining, and mold. Brands like Trex Enhance® and Select offer rich color palettes and natural wood grain at an approachable price point. Ideal for busy families.

Ultra-Premium Capped Polymer

The pinnacle of composite technology: TimberTech AZEC®, Trex Transcend®, and similar. Exceptional scratch resistance, the most realistic wood grain, and proprietary heat-reduction technology. Backed by 25-to-50-year warranties — perfect for Colorado’s intense sun and freeze-thaw cycles.

Eco-Friendly & Sustainable

Most composite decking is crafted from recycled wood fibers and reclaimed plastic — diverting waste from landfills. Low carbon footprint, no chemical treatments, and fully recyclable. Beautiful, responsible, and built to outlast wood 3x over.
