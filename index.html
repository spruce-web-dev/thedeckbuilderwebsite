Our Story

About The Deck Builder

The Deck Builder of Colorado is locally owned and operated in Littleton, Colorado by Richard Eva. We have been providing our professional design and construction services for decks, pergolas, patio covers, and more since 1992. Our mission is to create your backyard oasis where outdoor living in Colorado is at its best.

Our team takes pride in every project, using premium materials and proven techniques to create outdoor spaces our customers love.
