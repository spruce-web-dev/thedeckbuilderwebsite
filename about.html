OUR STORY

Locally Owned, Professionally Built

The Deck Builder of Colorado is locally owned and operated in Littleton, Colorado by Richard Eva. Since 1992, we've been providing professional design and construction services for decks, pergolas, patio covers, and more. Our mission is simple: create your backyard oasis where outdoor living in Colorado is at its best.

We take pride in our work. It's how we show our expertise and attention to detail and quality in every project. Richard still visits every job site personally, ensuring that each deck meets his high standards.
