Wood Deck Materials: Beauty + Performance

The material you choose defines your deck’s longevity, maintenance needs, and visual appeal. We guide you through each option, balancing aesthetics with Colorado’s high-altitude demands.

Western Red Cedar

Naturally rot-resistant, lightweight, and rich in warm tones. Cedar ages to a distinguished silver-gray if left untreated, or can be stained to preserve its honey glow. A classic choice for mountain and foothills homes.

Ipe & Tropical Hardwoods

Exceptionally dense, fire-resistant, and nearly impervious to insects. Ipe (Brazilian Walnut) offers deep chocolate hues and unmatched durability—perfect for high-traffic areas and extreme UV exposure.

Pressure-Treated Southern Pine

A cost-effective, resilient option that resists rot and decay. Modern treatment methods make it safer and more stable than ever. Ideal for framing and for homeowners who plan to stain or paint.
